Zakat due (2.5%)
Zakat rate is 2.5% of total zakatable assets above the nisab threshold.
Nisab is based on 85g of gold or 595g of silver — the lower value is used (silver nisab).
Property you live in, vehicles for personal use, and household items are not zakatable.
