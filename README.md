# Islamic Zakat Calculator 🕌

A free online Zakat calculator built for Muslims around the world.

## What is Zakat?
Zakat is one of the Five Pillars of Islam. Every Muslim who owns 
wealth above the Nisab threshold must pay 2.5% of their total 
zakatable wealth as Zakat each year.

## Features
- 10 currency options (PKR, USD, SAR, EUR, GBP, AED, BDT, INR, MYR, IDR)
- Gold and silver Zakat calculation
- Cash and bank savings calculation
- Property and rental income calculation
- Business assets and investments
- Automatic Nisab threshold calculation
- Deductions for debts and liabilities
- Full wealth breakdown

## How to Use
1. Select your currency
2. Enter current gold and silver prices
3. Fill in your assets (gold, silver, cash, property)
4. Enter any debts or liabilities
5. Click Calculate My Zakat
6. See your Zakat amount instantly

## Islamic Principles Used
- Nisab based on 85g of gold or 595g of silver
- Zakat rate is 2.5% of net zakatable wealth
- Liabilities are deducted before calculation
